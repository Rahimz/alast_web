According to the narration of the day when God made his covenant, there was light and then matter; The light of existence is imagined to be Lime Green and the darkness is a deep and dark Navy Blue. In analogy, existence is immeasurable and matter is small. The letter “A” is chosen to symbolize ALAST and D to symbolize the function of "DESIGN". After creating the matter, the light is trapped in the cage of the matter after its descent to the world, which must be dissipated and re-released through the channel of creativity. These two letters “AD” sit together so that the roots are not forgotten so that the material lasts and the origin of good thoughts, words and deeds remains green to the Last Day. Alast Design logo is the vision and functional manifesto for Alast Design Company.
